Project Overview

MATTRI AI is a revolutionary mental health co-pilot designed specifically for astronauts during space missions. Our solution addresses the unique psychological challenges faced by crew members in isolated, confined, and extreme environments.

Developed in response to Problem Statement ID: 25175 for the Smart India Hackathon 2025, our system provides proactive psychological support through multimodal AI analysis of vital signs, voice patterns, facial expressions, and behavioral indicators.

Key features include:

  • Continuous health monitoring through wearables and sensors
  • AI-powered stress and fatigue detection
  • CBT-informed conversational support
  • Privacy-first design with offline processing
  • Integration with ISRO's Gaganyaan mission requirements

The system is designed to support astronauts' mental well-being throughout extended space missions, ensuring mission success and crew safety.

Health Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of vital signs, voice patterns, and behavioral indicators to detect stress and fatigue early.

CBT-Informed AI

Conversational AI based on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy principles for meaningful psychological support.

Privacy-First Design

Offline-first processing with anonymized data storage to protect astronaut privacy during missions.

Mission Analytics

Comprehensive reporting and analytics for mission control to monitor crew well-being and mission success factors.
